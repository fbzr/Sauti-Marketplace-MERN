Proposal

- What problem does your app solve?
Limited reach due to geographic limitations as well as not knowing what the fair market price for products is.
- Be as specific as possible; how does your app solve the problem? 
Being able to allow vendors to reach a broader audience or clients for their products, as well as aligning them to be able to view product prices.
- What is the mission statement?  
To empower local vendors to better market their products and reach a broader customer base. 

Features

- What features are required for your minimum viable product?
	- A small business owner can log on and see relevant prices in various      categories to help them set their own prices. 
- They can also make listings for what they want to sell, which will show up to all users.
